The Starting Point
01

The Honest Picture

Who you are, what you've built, how you show up today.

Who They Are

Ugly John's Custom Boats is a multi-location marine dealership and full-service marina operating across Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Texas. They sell boats, rent and own marina slips, offer financing, run a service department, and handle rentals — a full-stack marine operation.

What They've Built

Five locations across Grand Lake, Tulsa, Beaver Lake, Lake Eufaula, and Lake Travis make Ugly John's one of the more geographically diverse marine dealers in the mid-south. Their guaranteed-slip program — where boat buyers receive a marina slip at purchase — is a competitive differentiator almost no other dealer can match.

How They Show Up

The website runs on DealerSpike, a marine industry template platform that puts inventory online but sacrifices brand differentiation. Their strongest sales argument — the guaranteed slip — is not surfaced above the fold, in the hero, or prominently in the purchase journey. Most new buyers searching online won't find it in time.

The review gap

With 35 reviews and a 4.1-star rating on Google, the customers who do find Ugly John's tend to like them. That's actually the problem. In a high-ticket category where buyers read dozens of reviews before calling anyone, 35 total across a 5-location operation is a signal that either the request isn't being made, or the follow-through isn't there. At this review volume, buyers hesitate. Competitors with 500+ reviews get the call first.

The opportunity is clear: Ugly John's has a real competitive advantage — the guaranteed slip — and a loyal customer base that keeps coming back. What they don't have is a digital presence that reflects any of it. Fixing that gap is how you stop handing sales to Prairie Creek.
