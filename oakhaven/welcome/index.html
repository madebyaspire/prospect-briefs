Real prose AI engines can lift

Hole-by-hole notes, banquet-hall details, instructor bios, course history. AI search rewards specificity — the more concrete the page, the more often it gets cited.

301-redirect every old URL to the new one. Suddenly the AI engines see one Oakhaven instead of two — and the authority you've already earned starts working for the right site.
