05The Future of Search

Google is no longer just ten blue links. AI-powered search (Google’s AI Overviews), voice assistants, and conversational search are fundamentally changing how patients find dental care. Here’s where Smile Center Ohio stands — and what needs to change.

What AI Search Looks For

  • Structured data (schema markup) — AI search engines rely on structured data to understand your business. When someone asks “find a dentist in Delaware OH that does implants and accepts MetLife,” Google needs schema markup to match you to that query. Currently, your site appears to lack Dentist, LocalBusiness, and MedicalOrganization schema.
  • E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust) — Google wants to know who wrote your content and why they’re credible. Your doctor bios mention fellowship credentials but don’t link to verifying organizations. Adding author attribution and credential verification would boost your E-E-A-T signals significantly.
  • Conversational content — Voice searches are usually questions: “Hey Google, who’s the best dentist near Sunbury?” or “Can I get dental implants the same day?” Your site answers some of these questions in the service pages, but they’re buried in paragraphs. FAQ sections with clear Q&A formatting would make this content accessible to both voice and AI search.

What’s Missing

  • No FAQ schema — FAQPage structured data would help your service pages appear in Google’s “People Also Ask” boxes and AI Overviews
  • No practitioner schema — Each doctor should have their own Physician/Dentist schema with credentials, education, and specialties listed
  • Location schema is absent or incomplete — Three separate LocalBusiness entries should be present, one per office, with accurate hours, addresses, and phone numbers
  • No review aggregation in search — Proper AggregateRating schema could display your star ratings directly in search results
  • YouTube channel underutilized — You have a YouTube channel (37 subscribers, 160 videos, 121K views) that’s barely integrated into the website. Video content is increasingly favored by AI search
Voice search reality: A growing number of patients — especially those searching for emergency dental care — use voice search. “Hey Siri, find an emergency dentist open near me” is a real query. Your after-hours emergency monitoring is a genuine advantage, but right now, voice search has no way to surface that because the structured data doesn’t exist.

06Here’s What We’d Actually Do

Based on our research, here are the five highest-impact improvements we’d recommend for Smile Center Ohio. These aren’t generic suggestions — they’re specific to your practice, your market, and your competitive landscape.

1. Custom Multi-Location Website

Replace the Duda template with a custom-designed, high-performance website built for conversion. Each of your three locations gets a dedicated, SEO-optimized landing page with unique content, embedded maps, staff photos, and location-specific testimonials. The design would reflect the premium, advanced-technology feel of your practice — not a generic dental template.

High ImpactMedium Effort

2. Schema Markup & Technical SEO

Implement comprehensive structured data across the entire site: LocalBusiness (per location), Dentist schema, FAQPage schema on service pages, AggregateRating from your Google reviews, and Physician schema for each doctor. Fix the duplicate content issues and correct alt text errors (goodbye, Tampa). This alone could significantly improve your search visibility.

High ImpactQuick Win

3. Content Marketing Engine

Launch a blog targeting patient questions and local search terms. Start with pillar content: “Complete Guide to Dental Implants in Delaware County,” “Understanding Orthodontic Options for Adults,” and “What to Expect During Sedation Dentistry.” Each piece links to your service pages and targets the long-tail keywords your competitors are already ranking for.

High ImpactOngoing

4. Brand Unification Campaign

Align all online listings under the “Smile Center Ohio” brand. Update your Facebook page name (still “Delaware Smile Center”), reconcile Yelp listings, update Google Business Profiles, and ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all directories. Inconsistent branding confuses both patients and search engines.

Medium ImpactQuick Win

5. Review Generation System

You have great reviews, but not enough of them. Delaware Family Dental claims 200+ five-star reviews. Aspen Dental has 626 reviews on Birdeye alone. Implementing an automated post-visit review request system (via text or email) could conservatively double your review volume within 6 months, directly improving both your local pack ranking and patient trust signals.

08Beyond the Website

A great website is the foundation, but there are additional channels where Smile Center Ohio can grow its patient base and deepen relationships with existing patients:

📍 Google Business Optimization

Three locations = three separate GBP listings that need individual attention. Proper category selection, photo updates, Q&A management, and post scheduling for each office. Your North Orange location in particular needs more photos and reviews.

📱 Social Media Strategy

Your Facebook (4.4K followers) is active but your Instagram (690 followers) has room to grow. Short-form video content — team intros, patient smile reveals, behind-the-scenes — would perform well on both platforms and build the personal connection patients crave.

📧 Email & SMS Recall

Automated appointment reminders, recall campaigns for patients overdue for cleanings, and seasonal promotions (back-to-school check-ups, New Year’s smile makeover campaigns) could reduce no-shows and reactivate dormant patients.

⭐ Review Automation

Implement a system that sends a friendly text or email after each appointment with a direct link to leave a Google review. With three locations seeing patients daily, even a modest response rate would dramatically increase your review count within months.

🎥 YouTube Revitalization

You have 160 videos and 121K views — that’s a real asset. Refreshing the channel with updated branding, creating a content series around common patient questions, and embedding these videos throughout the new website would boost both SEO and patient engagement.

📅 Online Booking Upgrade

Your current per-location forms work, but a true online scheduling system that lets patients see available slots and book instantly — without a phone call — could significantly increase new patient conversions, especially for younger demographics.
