05The Future of Search

Here's something most flooring companies aren't even thinking about yet: the way people search is changing fast.

Instead of typing into Google, more and more people are asking questions out loud to Siri, Alexa, or Google Assistant. Or they're asking ChatGPT, "Who should I hire for hardwood flooring in Columbus?" And getting a direct answer — no scrolling through ten blue links.

This is called AI-powered search, and it's growing every month. Here's the problem:

Right now, if someone asks an AI assistant "who's the best flooring installer near Columbus, Ohio?" — Tee's Flooring won't show up. Not because you're not great at what you do. Because your website doesn't speak the language these tools understand.

What AI Search Looks For

Think of AI assistants like a really smart friend who reads every website and remembers everything. They recommend businesses that make it easy to understand:

  • What you do — clear, detailed descriptions of your services (your service pages are too thin right now)
  • Where you work — your 13 location pages are a great start, but they need more unique content
  • Why you're trustworthy — your A+ BBB and great reviews help, but they need to be structured so AI can read them
  • How to reach you — your click-to-call is great, but there's no online scheduling for consultations

What's Missing (The Technical Stuff)

Don't worry about memorizing these terms — this is the behind-the-scenes stuff we handle. But it's worth knowing about:

  • "Schema markup" — this is invisible code that tells Google and AI assistants: "This is a flooring company. They're located here. They offer these services. Here are their reviews." Your site doesn't have any. Your competitors do.
  • "E-E-A-T" — stands for Experience, Expertise, Authority, and Trust. Google uses this to decide who to recommend. You score high on Experience (20 years!) and Trust (BBB A+), but your website doesn't showcase your Expertise well enough.
  • Fresh content — AI tools prefer websites that are regularly updated. Your blog hasn't been touched since July 2024.
Bottom line: The businesses that get ahead of AI search now will dominate for the next decade. We'd make sure Tee's Flooring is one of them. 🐾
