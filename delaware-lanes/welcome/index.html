Who They Are

Delaware Lanes has been the community's bowling anchor for 60+ years — a locally owned, non-franchise institution at 536 Sunbury Rd in the heart of Delaware, Ohio. Not a chain. Not a concept. A real place that real people have been coming back to for generations.

What They've Built

293 Google reviews at 4.3 stars — real community trust earned over decades. The official home court for the Olentangy Berlin Bears high school bowling team. A full bar, concessions (burgers, pizza, fries), and a league program that keeps the lanes full all week long.

How They Show Up

The website runs on free Google Sites — no SEO control, no booking, minimal mobile optimization. Open bowling schedules aren't listed online; visitors must call. Social media presence is minimal while the closest competitor has 2,000+ Facebook followers.

The Plan

Here's What We'd Actually Do

Specific, prioritized recommendations — not a generic checklist.

Quick Win
Start this week — no website needed
Optimize Google Business Profile — Add Photos, Categories & Attributes

Your GBP has 293 reviews but is likely missing photos of the bar, food, and arcade. Adding 20+ quality photos, updating your category to include "Bar" and "Restaurant," and filling in amenity attributes (full bar, food, arcade, birthday parties) immediately improves how you appear in local pack results. This costs nothing and takes a few hours. We can guide the process or do it directly.

Month 1
Core launch deliverables
Custom Website — Built for Conversion, Not Just Information

A new site built on Astro with full SEO control, fast load times, and a professional design that honors Delaware Lanes' history while appealing to new customers. Core pages: Home, Open Bowling, Leagues, Bar & Food, Birthday Parties, Contact.

Online Lane Reservation System

Integrate a booking widget so customers can check availability and reserve a lane without calling. Options range from a simple Calendly flow to dedicated bowling software embeds. Removes the #1 friction point for younger customers.

Bar & Food Menu Page with Photography

The bar is already "the best kept secret in Delaware" — let's stop keeping it secret. A dedicated menu page with real food photography, weekly specials, and featured drinks gives people a reason to visit even when they're not sure about bowling.

Birthday Party & Events Booking Page

A dedicated page optimized for "bowling birthday party Delaware Ohio" with pricing info, party packages, and an online inquiry form. This is the highest-revenue search term you're currently missing entirely — and it has immediate ROI potential.

Month 2+
Growth phase
Social Media Strategy + Review Growth Engine

A structured plan to grow Facebook and Instagram presence — league night photos, bar specials, team spotlights, and birthday party features. Paired with a review ask strategy (QR codes at the lanes, post-visit follow-up) to push toward 400+ reviews and widen the gap on competitors. Content that shows Delaware Lanes as the community hub it actually is.
